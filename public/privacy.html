Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

The short version

Chessuranga does not collect, store, or share your personal data. No accounts. No tracking. No nonsense.

Data Collection

What we collect

Chessuranga does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, or any other personal information.

Analytics

Anonymous usage data

The web version of Chessuranga (chessuranga.com) uses Vercel Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated usage data such as page visits. No personally identifiable information is collected or stored.

Daily Puzzle

Puzzle requests

The daily puzzle feature makes a request to our server solely to retrieve today's puzzle. No user data is sent or stored as part of this request.

Third Parties

External services

The app contains a link to Ko-fi for optional voluntary tips. Clicking this link opens an external website governed by Ko-fi's own privacy policy. We have no visibility into any data Ko-fi may collect.

Children

Suitable for all ages

Chessuranga is suitable for all ages and does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Contact

Get in touch

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via LinkedIn.
